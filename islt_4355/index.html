Soccer for Dummies

Welcome to your hub for information about the most popular sport in the world, soccer.

Goals of the Website

With the rise of soccer in the United States over the last few decades it is necessary for Americans to have a way to learn about the sport in a way that they can easily understand. With soccer being the number one sport in the world, most Americans will have some catching up to do as far as understanding the sport goes. This website aims to turn the difficult concepts of soccer into easy-to-understand ideas. Ideas that people are often confused about will be worded in a simple way. This website also hopes to increase the popularity of soccer in the United States as people are able to better understand it. With the recent rise in the popularity of soccer following the World Cup in 2022, more people are finding an interest in the sport which they know so little about. This website hopes to inform these people and provide them with the basic knowledge they need to be a fan of soccer.
